About

NCSU Nazaare was founded in 2012 by Meera Patel and Neelam Patel with the intention of bringing awareness to South-Asian culture on the NC State campus. We are a student run, co-ed competitive Bollywood-fusion dance team based in Raleigh, North Carolina. Our routines consist of various styles including Bollywood, hip-hop, bhangra, classical, kuthu, contemporary, and more. We combine intricate choreography with impressive technique and storytelling. In the past, we have brought the plots of movies, such as Inside Out, Black Swan, and Night at the Museum, to life through acting, dialogue, and set design. We have the honor of competing at some of the nationâ€™s most well-known Bollywood-fusion dance competitions each year against collegiate teams from all over the country. We have represented NC State University at national competitions several times. NCSU Nazaare strives to bring an entertaining and engaging performance to the stage every single year.
