2012-2013
During our first season, we competed at the Manhattan Project.
2013-2014
During the fall semester, we competed at Aaj Ka Dhamaka and received first place. During the spring semester, we competed at the Manhattan Project, East Coast Dhamaka, and Aag Ki Raat where we placed third, third, and second respectively.
2014-2015
During the fall semester, we competed at Jhalak. During the spring semester, we competed at The Manhattan Project and Aag ki Raat where we placed first.
2015-2016
During the fall semester, we attended Jhalak where we placed first place which gave us a direct bid to Bollywood America 2016. During the spring semester, we placed second at Legacy on Broad and third at A-Town Showdown. We attended a national competition (Bollywood America) where we placed 5th.
2016-2017
During the fall semester, we competed at Aaj ka Dhamaka and Jhalak where we placed second and third place, respectively. During the spring semester, we competed at A-Town Showdown where we placed second place, Midwest Dhamaka where we placed third, and Infusion. We also competed at Bollywood America, the national competition.
2017-2018
During the fall semester, we competed at Aaj ka Dhamaka where we received first place and at Jhalak where we received second place. During the spring semester, we competed at Midwest Dhamaka and A-Town Showdown where we placed third and second, respectively. We also had the honor of competing at a national competition (Legends)
2018-2019
During the fall semester, we competed at Aaj ka Dhamaka where we received second place. During the spring semester, we competed at nationals (Legends), as well as Maryland Minza and Aag Ki Raat where we placed first and second, respectively.
2019-2020
During the fall semester, we competed at Aaj ka Dhamaka. In the spring semester, we competed at Maryland Minza and UGA India Night where we received 3rd place.
